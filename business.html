Business Opportunities

LawnSurfing® SurfSlides are designed and built to maximize customer satisfaction, providing extended seasons of operations in a variety of climatic terrain environments. LawnSurfing® SurfSlides are ideally suited for lower mountain operations to utilize existing snowmaking and lift equipment, retail and ticketing systems, and general resort facilities. Surf slides fit into all kinds of terrain attributes. Below are a few sample designs to whet your appetite.

1. Amusement Pier

Surf slides can be formatted to fit into tight parameters if necessary. The following surfslide sketch is 400' X 40' designed to flow under a roller coaster on an amusement pier boardwalk

2. Ski Resort

The opportunity to implement surf slides on existing ski areas is endless. Ultimately designed to mimic natures terrain, surf slides offer the potential to give resort operators year-round revenues utilizing existing infrastructure, particularly in the lower mountain area with easier slopes and existing ski tows and snowmaking equipment.

3. Stand Alone Facility

As an opportunity to create a stand alone recreation center, the surfslides can be buried in snow for winter activities in the colder climate regions, or can offer 12 months of revenues in warmer locales. Use them as part of a larger operation, combined with water slides, skate parks, golf courses or batting cages, the surf slides will hold their own.

The19th Reef™

The LawnSurfing® 19th Reef™ is our franchise opportunity to bring surf slides to your facility. From systems engineered design and build contracting to retail development, The 19th Reef™ franchise will include surf slides custom fit to your terrain, SurfinTurf™ product lines for riding boards, active wear and allied products, and concession systems.

1. Investors

For those wishing to participate in the new sport of surf sliding, we are seeking qualified financiers in our goal of market penetration. Surf slides are protected by a series of patents in the US and abroad, trademarks and media entities, enabling us to capitalize with a market-ready bundle of goods. The surf slides will follow a similar path as that of snowboarding, but with a much greater opportunity for climatic regional development.

2. LawnSurfing® Development

We can creatively work with land owners in bringing surfslides to market. Whether a turnkey installation is preferred, or cooperative ownership is better, we will make the surf slides economically feasible for you.
